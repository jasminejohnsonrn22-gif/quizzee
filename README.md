# Jeopardy! AI Quiz

An unlimited, AI-powered Jeopardy practice quiz that generates fresh clues on demand and grades your answers with explanations.

## Features

- **Unlimited questions** — Claude generates a new clue every time, never repeating
- **7 categories** — History, Geography, Literature, Science & Nature, Pop Culture, Sports, or Mixed
- **5 difficulty levels** — $200 through $1000, or mixed
- **AI grading** — your answer is judged by Claude, with a full explanation whether you’re right or wrong
- **Score tracking** — correct, wrong, and current streak
- **No backend required** — runs entirely in the browser as a single HTML file

## How to Use

Open `jeopardy_quiz.html` in any modern browser. No installation or setup needed.

1. Select a **category** and **difficulty**
1. Click **Start Practicing** or **New Clue**
1. Read the clue and type your answer (phrased as a question — “Who is…?” or “What is…?”)
1. Press **Enter** or click **Submit Answer**
1. Claude grades your answer and explains the context
1. Click **Next Clue** to keep going
